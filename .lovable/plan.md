

# Voltiq Energy Website Plan

## Overview
A modern, professional one-page website for Voltiq Energy featuring a clean, futuristic design with a white background and green/blue energy-themed accents. The site will be fully responsive and include subtle animations for a polished user experience.

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: White background, vibrant green (eco/sustainability), electric blue (energy/innovation), with subtle grays for text
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif fonts with strong hierarchy
- **Style**: Minimal, futuristic, lots of white space, subtle shadows and gradients

---

## Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Sticky header with Voltiq Energy text logo
- Smooth scroll navigation links (About, Solutions, Why Us, Testimonials, Contact)
- "Get a Free Consultation" CTA button (highlighted in green)

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width banner with gradient background
- Large tagline: **"Powering a Smarter, Greener Future"**
- Subtitle introducing lithium inverters, solar, and storage solutions
- Prominent "Get a Free Consultation" CTA button
- Subtle fade-in animation on load

### 3. About Us
- Clean two-column layout (text + decorative energy illustration/icon)
- 2-3 paragraphs about Voltiq's mission for efficient, sustainable, uninterrupted energy
- Fade-in animation on scroll

### 4. Our Solutions
- Three cards in a responsive grid:
  - **Lithium Inverters** - Icon, description of smart control and long backup
  - **Solar Solutions** - Icon, rooftop and on-grid/off-grid systems
  - **Energy Storage Systems** - Icon, custom battery solutions for commercial use
- Hover effects with subtle scale animation

### 5. Why Choose Voltiq
- Icon-based feature grid (6 items in 2-3 columns):
  - High Efficiency
  - Long Life
  - Eco-Friendly
  - After-Sales Support
  - 5-Year Warranty Options
  - 24/7 Service
- Each with an icon and short description

### 6. Testimonials
- 3 customer reviews with avatar placeholders, names, and roles
- Carousel or grid layout
- Sample reviews from homeowner, office owner, and small business owner

### 7. Contact Section
- Clean, centered layout displaying contact information only
- Contact details displayed prominently: Phone, Email, Address
- Social media icon links (WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook)
- "Get a Free Consultation" CTA button linking to phone/email
- **No form** - just clear contact information for visitors to reach out
